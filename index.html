DBLP

I am a Doctoral Scholar and Research Assistant at Department of Computer Science, University of California, Davis. I started by PhD at Purdue University, and transferred to UC Davis in January 2018. I am working under the guidance of Dr. Mohammad Sadoghi. My current research interests are at the intersection of Distributed Databases and Systems. I am also interested in the design of fault tolerant blochchain systems.

I hold two master degrees from Purdue University and Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I was awarded Master of Science in Computer Science by Purdue University in December 2017. During my master's at Purdue I also worked with Prof. Suresh Jagannathan in the domain of Distributed Systems and Programming Languages, and was associated with. associated with the PurPL and SIAM.

I received another Master of Science degree from Department of CSE, Indian Institute of Technology Madras in July 2015. I was advised by Dr. V. Krishna Nandivada. My thesis was titled as Analyzing Recursive Task Parallel Programs.
