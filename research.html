SUYASH GUPTA

I am a Doctoral Scholar and Research Assistant at Department of Computer Science, Purdue University. I work under the guidance of Prof. Suresh Jagannathan. My current research interests are at the intersection of Distributed Systems and Programming Languages. I am also associated with the PurPL and SIAM at Purdue.

I received my Master of Science degree from Department of CSE, Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I was advised by Dr. V. Krishna Nandivada. My thesis was titled as Analyzing Recursive Task Parallel Programs.
